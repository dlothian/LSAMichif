Move With Michif

Come and play Kokum Ehitwet! (Kokum Says!) To play, you have to do what Kokum tells you, but only if she tells you in Michif! If you do what she says when she tells you in English, you're out! And don't forget to nakî (stop!) when she says! Here are some ideas to get you started!
